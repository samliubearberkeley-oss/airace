# AI Logo Resources

This document lists official logo resources for each AI model used in the AI Maze Race application.

## Logo Sources

### 1. OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-5)
- **Official Brand Assets**: https://openai.com/brand
- **Wikimedia Commons**: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:OpenAI_Logo.svg
- **Simple Icons**: https://simpleicons.org/icons/openai.svg
- **Download PNG**: Convert SVG from Wikimedia Commons or use Simple Icons

### 2. Anthropic Claude (Claude Sonnet 4.5)
- **Official Website**: https://www.anthropic.com
- **Brand Guidelines**: Check Anthropic's official brand resources
- **Logo**: Usually available in their press kit or brand assets page

### 3. Google Gemini (Gemini 2.5 Pro)
- **Google Brand Resources**: https://about.google/brand-resource-center/
- **Gemini Brand**: Check Google AI/Gemini official pages
- **Simple Icons**: https://simpleicons.org/icons/google.svg (for Google logo)

### 4. xAI Grok (Grok 4)
- **xAI Official**: https://x.ai
- **Brand Assets**: Check xAI's official website or press resources
- **Note**: xAI is relatively new, logo may be available on their official site

## Recommended Download Methods

### Method 1: Using Simple Icons (Easiest)
Simple Icons provides SVG logos for many brands. You can convert SVG to PNG:
- Visit: https://simpleicons.org/
- Search for: OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, xAI
- Download SVG and convert to PNG using online tools

### Method 2: Wikimedia Commons
- Visit: https://commons.wikimedia.org/
- Search for brand names
- Download SVG/PNG files
- Most are public domain or CC licensed

### Method 3: Official Brand Assets Pages
- Check each company's official brand/logo page
- Usually provides high-quality PNG/SVG files
- May require account or approval

## Logo Specifications Needed

For the application, we need:
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Size**: At least 64x64px, preferably 128x128px or larger
- **Style**: Square or circular format preferred
- **Background**: Transparent
