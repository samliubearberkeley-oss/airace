# Quick Logo Download Links

## Direct PNG Download Sources

### OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-5)
- **Simple Icons CDN**: https://cdn.simpleicons.org/openai/10A37F/128/128
- **Wikimedia Commons**: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:OpenAI_Logo.svg (convert to PNG)
- **Logo Search**: https://logos-world.net/openai-logo/

### Anthropic Claude
- **Logo Search**: https://logos-world.net/anthropic-logo/
- **Google Images**: Search "Anthropic logo PNG transparent"
- **Alternative**: Check Anthropic's official website brand assets

### Google Gemini
- **Simple Icons CDN**: https://cdn.simpleicons.org/google/4285F4/128/128
- **Google Brand Center**: https://about.google/brand-resource-center/
- **Gemini Specific**: Search "Google Gemini logo PNG"

### xAI Grok
- **Logo Search**: https://logos-world.net/xai-logo/
- **Official Site**: https://x.ai (check brand assets)
- **Twitter/X**: Check @xAI official account

## Online SVG to PNG Converters

1. **CloudConvert**: https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png
2. **Convertio**: https://convertio.co/svg-png/
3. **Online-Convert**: https://image.online-convert.com/convert-to-png

## Recommended Logo Sites

1. **Logos World**: https://logos-world.net/ (has many brand logos)
2. **SeekPNG**: https://www.seekpng.com/ (PNG logos)
3. **PNGWing**: https://www.pngwing.com/ (transparent PNGs)
4. **Simple Icons**: https://simpleicons.org/ (SVG, need conversion)

## Quick Steps

1. Visit the links above
2. Download PNG files (preferably 128x128px or larger)
3. Ensure transparent background
4. Save to `public/logos/` directory
5. Name files: `openai.png`, `anthropic.png`, `google.png`, `xai.png`
